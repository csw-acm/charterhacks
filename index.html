CharterHacks 2021!

Want to grow your skills in programming, or are interested in building apps and games? Join us over August 6-9 in CharterHacks! In this Hackathon, you have the chance to harness your skills and make friends while creating amazing projects. Sign up and experience the weekend event with fun events and games where you can meet other like-minded programmers! Best of all, win amazing prizes with your astounding projects!

FAQ

Information

CharterHacks is a hackathon run by students in the Charter School of Wilmington. In this hackathon, we hope to bring the brightest students from around the world to participate in a weekend hackathon with many fun events, games, and prizes to keep everyone entertained! If you have any questions about this hackathon, you can email us at cswhackathon@gmail.com or talk with us through Discord.

Submissions

You are given August 6-9th to create a project in a team of 1-4. Submissions and additional information can be found on DevPost. In your submission, you will include a presentation of your project that will show your idea in motion while answering other important questions such as challenges you had to overcome during creation, what your project does, and its audience.

Grading

Grading will be fairly simple. You will be graded based on creativity, complexity, and the originality of your idea. The rubric is found here. Plagiarism in this contest will not be tolerated, though website templates and use of code snippets will be allowed (with given credit in the code). Your goal shouldn't be to win, but just to have fun!

FAQ

Who can participate?

As long as you are a high-schooler or middle-schooler, you can! This means that even if you graduated this school year, you may still participate.

What if I can't code?

Coding isn't as hard as many people think it is. With a little time and effort, you can become an amazing coder! You can check out our resources here for some help.

When will the hackathon be held?

The hackathon will be held on July 17-20th. If you start working on your project beforehand, it will not be considered for judging.

Why should I do this hackathon?

You should participate in the hackathon because it will be fun. Not to mention, there will be prizes for the winners and minicontests as well!

Does it cost money to compete?

No. Participating in the hackathon will be absolutely free to everyone thanks to our amazing sponsors found here!

What workshops will be held?

We will hold Python, Machine Learning, and Web Development Workshops. You can see our whole schedule and other logistics here.
